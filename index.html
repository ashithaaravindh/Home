What is ABA ?
To give the idea, in Indian context it is an advanced science of Psychology, although it is not psychology any more. Applied behavioral analysis (ABA) is a type of therapy that can improve social, communication, and learning skills through positive reinforcement. Many experts consider ABA to be the gold-standard treatment for children with autism spectrum disorder (ASD) or any other developmental conditions or delay . (https://www.healthline.com/health/aba-therapy)

How do you Teach
I use scientific procedures , based on research from the field of ABA. Reinforcers or positive consequences known to have positive effects in learning. Similarly we tend to positively reinforce a lot of unwanted behaviours too. Using scientific tools we can figure out how things are learned, and how to unlearn and relearn the right behaviours. I develop educational and or behavior plans for the students and work with them for 3 months. These goals are specific to individual needs. Based on an initial assessment the plan will be developed and all the plans and development of the program given via online folders. After each 3 months based on the progress and discussions with parents further decisions are taken.
